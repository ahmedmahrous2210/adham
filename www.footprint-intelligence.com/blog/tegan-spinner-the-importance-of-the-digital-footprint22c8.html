August 9, 2022 Sebastian Gier

Tegan Spinner

Founder & CEO of Worthmore

Tegan has spent the last five years working with some of the best startups in Denmark. Most specifically, as CTO of Zentabox, the personalized vitamins, and supplements company, and Marketing manager at Opbeat, an open-source application performance monitoring tool for developers.

Through his role as a Nordic Talent Ambassador and Startup Denmark Visa Consultant, he actively works on attracting and helping entrepreneurs from around the world find their way around the Danish startup ecosystem by sharing my knowledge and experience.

Currently, he is the CEO and Founder at Worthmore. Worthmore aims to be the first carbon-neutral mobile service provider for companies and individuals.
