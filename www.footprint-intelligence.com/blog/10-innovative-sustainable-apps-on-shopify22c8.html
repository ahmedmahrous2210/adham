10 Innovative Sustainable Apps on Shopify.

November 8, 2021 Sebastian Gier

Noor Ashraf

Graduated in Bachelor of Science in Public Health - Footprint Marketing and Fashion Expert.

These Shopify apps offer tools and advanced features that will make eCommerce, data organization, CRM, shipping and logistics stream-lined and simple for better customer satisfaction. There are 5,000 apps on Shopify that can help you in this area. We have already spent time selecting the ones that will provide you most value, so you don't need to spent endless time searching for them.

1. ProPickup: Store Pickup

Use the PropPickup: Store Pickup app to offer multiple shipping methods for customers to choose from. Customers are able to schedule their specific pickup date and time, whichever conveniently suits their needs.

ProPickup

2. Matrixify

Matrixify is a helpful tool that can manage your Shopify store data. Some key features Matrixify offers is backing up your whole shop, exporting your data to other platforms such as Google Marketplace, and copying your data to your other stores with only one zip or Excel file.

Matrixify

3. ReStock: Back in Stock Alerts

Customers are easily able to subscribe to any out-of-stock items and they will receive emails as well as browser notifications that will notify the customer once that product is restocked. Some advanced features include: automatic/manual back in stock notification, multi-lingual widgets, and feature request and customize services.

ReStock

4. AfterShip Order Tracking & SMS

With AfterShip Order Tracking & SMS, automatically import tracking numbers into AfterShip which allows customers to get current tracking info for all shipments in one place. Delays, current order statuses, and sending delivering notifications via email & SMS is all possible with this app.

AfterShip

5. Judge.me Product Reviews

Judge.me helps collect star ratings as well as reviews about your Shopify store and products. Buyer engagement, organic traffic, and conversion rates can be be increased with user-generated content such as photos and videos. Good news: products and videos are free.

Judge.me

6. Sales Countdown Timer Bar 2.0

A countdown timer can be completely customized for important days such as: Holiday seasons, Black Friday, Christmas, flash sales, special offers, free shipping and much more. Use the Sales Countdown Timer Bar 2.0 app to personalize background patterns and choose from 11 design templates.

Sales Countdown Timer Bar 2.0

7. Shippo

With the Shippo app, optimize cash flow by only paying for labels when you print, integrate your existing branded experience, and create labels quickly with saved and pre-filled package information.

Shippo

8. WinkSprout

Drive traffic to your website and scale and promote your products on Instagram through posts and stories. Boost best-selling items and new products in order to promote new product campaigns.

WinkSprout

9. Easy FAQ Page | Help Support

Modern FAQ pages can be generated for your store. FAQ pages are customizable and fonts, styles, and colors can be altered. Some features include: modern customizable templates, displaying FAQs on any page, exporting FAQs, and customizing CSS.

Easy FAQ Page

10. Gift Box- BOGO & Free Gifts

Using Gift Boxes will help create your own free gift setup which will help encourage and reward your customers. Spend ten dollars more in order to get free shipping, gift card, gift wrap, or free products.

Gift Box

From data management, to customer service and customizing CSS... these Shopify apps can help increase customer engagement, increase brand awareness, and are easily installable. This will not only benefit your business, but will save plenty of time too!
