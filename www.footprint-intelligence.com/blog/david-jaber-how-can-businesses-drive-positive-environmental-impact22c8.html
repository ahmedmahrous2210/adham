David Jaber on “How can businesses drive positive environmental impact”.

August 2, 2022 Sebastian Gier

David Jaber

Founder of Climate Positive Consulting

David Jaber is the Founder of Climate Positive Consulting, a firm that advances better decision-making through climate strategy, carbon footprint analytics, and facility site assessments to drive greenhouse gas benefits and reduction. Over the course of twenty years, David has worked with over 150 companies on environmental excellence. He is author of the book “Climate Positive Business: How You and Your Company Hit Bold Climate Goals and Go Net Zero, an advisory board member at Planet FWD, and a Project Drawdown Fellow.

In this episode of the Footprint Futures podcast, we talk with David Jaber, Founder of Climate Positive Consulting, on how can businesses drive positive environmental impact.

During the episode we approach:

  • The need for greenhouse gas reduction strategies

  • How corporates sustainability awareness, consumer, and demand have been involved in the past 20 years

  • ESG as a metric.
