Helena Lindemark | Can considering biodiversity help us solve the climate crisis?

August 3, 2020 Sebastian Gier

Helena Lindemark

Sustainability Change Agent and founder, Sustainable Development Sweden AB and 2022 Initiative Foundation

In our second episode, we have invited Helena Lindemark as our guest to talk about biodiversity and climate change. Helena has been working with sustainability since 1991 at the UN, EU, Swedish Sida and the private sector. She is the founder of Sustainable Development Sweden and the 2022 Initiative Foundation, focusing on speeding up sustainability. Helena is also a Climate Reality Leader, trained by Al Gore.
