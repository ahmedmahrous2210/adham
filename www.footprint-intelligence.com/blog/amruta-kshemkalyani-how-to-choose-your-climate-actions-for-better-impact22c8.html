Amruta Kshemkalyani | How to choose your climate actions for better impact?

July 31, 2020 Sebastian Gier

Amruta Kshemkalyani

Sustainability advisor, speaker and trainer

Founder of Middle East's pioneering digital platform

Founder of ZeroWasteUAE Social initiative

How to choose your climate actions for better impact? In our first interview episode, Amruta Kshemkalyani has given us useful advice. With 14 years of experience of helping businesses with sustainability transformation & implementation of sustainability, Amruta Kshemkalyani worked on various sustainable development projects in UAE & Qatar as a sustainability project manager at design as well as construction stage.

As sustainability advisor, she has been assisting organizations with businesses sustainability to achieve their short & long term goals and to create more positive impacts. As Sustainability Speaker & Trainer, she has trained thousands of professionals & executives for different aspects of sustainability throughout her career. She founded Middle East's pioneering digital platform with the mission of raising public awareness & created an audience, their Tribe of like-minded people over the last 11 years by educating them about Sustainable Living & Conscious Consumption.

Additionally, she also founded #ZeroWasteUAE Social Initiative, which sparked zero waste movement in the UAE. She regularly holds awareness campaigns, educational workshops, community events & talks. Via Sustainability Tribe platforms, she also helps businesses with Sustainability Reporting, Communication & Marketing.
