The principles we stand for

01 — Science first.

At the core of our work is the impact we create, and we consistently incorporate the most current findings from environmental research into our approach.

02 — Greenwashing free.

Greenwashing is one of the core problems of sustainability programs in the business world. We are driven to empower organizations to act without greenwashing.

03 — Inclusivity empowered.

Footprint Intelligence is designed to make sustainability inclusive. Employees and customers are the key to a sustainable transformation.

04 — Change accelerated.

The world is moving towards big problems, so we aim to accelerate the change towards a brighter future with tools that empower organization to act.
